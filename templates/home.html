Frequently Asked Questions

How does the system ensure tracking accuracy?

The system uses YOLOv8 for detection and DeepSORT for tracking. This combination allows us to maintain the identity of an individual even if they are momentarily blocked from view.

Can I customize the safety thresholds?

Yes, you can define specific capacity limits for every zone you create. The system will trigger an alert the moment a limit is reached.

Is my profile information secure?

Absolutely. We use secure password hashing and JWT-based session management to ensure your data is always protected.

What video sources can I use?

You can upload MP4/AVI video files or use a live webcam feed to define and monitor your zones.
